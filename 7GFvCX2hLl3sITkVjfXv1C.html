About

20 monthly listeners
Big Homie G is a mexican Hip-Hop artist and executive producer. He’s known as G in the streets. His birth name is Adan Medina and was born September 16, 1982 which is “El Dia Del Grito” Mexico’s Independence Day. The G in his name stands for “Grito”. Merced, Ca is his birth place while raised in a Country town of Winton, Ca where he first picked up the microphone and started free styling to instrumentals back in the early 90’s just flowing and not stopping at all through elementary, middle school, and high school just being the life of the party taking over crowds. Back then it was hard for a independent rap artist to get into a live studio. Now he’s the executive producer of Illuminated Music, Illuminated Digital Music, and Mental Level Music Entertainment. Started his career in 2007 but began rapping at a young age till he dropped his first mixtape called “Mixtape Maddness Vol. 1”. Started new projects and other songs in 2010. Finally released first album in 2017 named “Life Within Lyrics”. In 2019 released “ Ahead Of The Class” and In 2020 he released first compilation with various artists “Two Zero Nine”. Plus four singles that year including “Abanded Lil Child”, “Lil Homie”, “Step Into Our World feat. Overdose”, and “It’s Hard feat. Supper Deezy”. 2021 was a ruff year for G but still released “They All Ready Know feat. Sickwitit” & “Keep It Real feat. Halfminded”. His career is not over yet
much more music to come as his music skills are elevating legandary!
